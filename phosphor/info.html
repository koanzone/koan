PHOSPHOR

Info Demo

What it is

PHOSPHOR is a portable, autonomous lighting rig for small/medium venues. It plugs into your DMX via Art-Net/sACN, listens to the room, and renders four coherent scenes—Ambient, Build, Drop, Outro—that track the music and camera view in real time. No operator needed.

How it works

  • Raspberry Pi + touch display + camera; outputs Art-Net/sACN.
  • Audio features (loudness/onsets) + vision features (room hue/motion) drive color, intensity, and pacing.
  • Designed to patch quickly into house rigs; supports DMX PARs and WLED/Hypercube-style LEDs.

Pilot night offer

We’ll do a discounted pilot night. If you’re not into it after the first hour, we pack up—no charge. Typical nights: $250–$400 depending on scope.

What we need from you

  • One free DMX universe (we bring our own Art-Net node; sACN available).
  • One standard power outlet near FOH or stage.
  • Access to the house patch (fixtures, addresses); we map on arrival.
  • Optional: a booth mic feed; otherwise we use our own mic.

Setup & reliability

  • Setup: under 15 minutes; labeled cabling; auto-diagnostics on boot.
  • Safety: strobe duty-cycle limits; blackout hotkey; quick bypass back to your desk control.
  • Footprint: carry-on case + small camera mount; minimal stage clutter.
