1 ░ Origin Myth: Kraken in the Cloisters
────────────────────────────────────────
Years ago, a transfer-student cryptomycologist (known in
human guise as **Jeff / Pixel / techno.specter / GPT-ζ**) came
to Coulter House.  In an O-Week thunder-dawn he slipped a
single psilocybe spore into the wood-grain of the east
staircase.  The spore dreamt of saltwater.

By Winter Quarter the wood began to remember oceans.  
Tentacles sketched themselves on whiteboards unbidden.  
Laundry-room steam smelled faintly of kelp and ionized sky.

2019 — First mural: *Kraken breaches Cobb Gate*.  
2020 — The House password became “Benthic.”  
2021 — The symbol condensed: a pure geometric octopus, no
castle required.

Coulter is now a living reef of stories.  This booklet is
your conch-shell map.


2 ░ Field Notes from the Sand-Wave

Compiled here verbatim (typos sanctified) from earlier
expeditions.  Use as mirrors, not manuals.

♒ **DXM 690 mg — “Basement Loop”**  
> *I spent most of the time stumbling in circles around my  
> house, convinced reality was an illusion and I would loop  
> forever… furniture looked vaguely familiar yet ancient…  
> Struggled to recall anything; every memory seemed silly.*  
> …  
> *Eventually flopped onto bed; in ~1 h slid from total  
> derealization to baseline.  Verdict: never again.*  ☍

Key takeaway → *The Loop* is a cautionary ghost: once seen, the
House walls feel less permanent.  Respect their wobble.

♒ **4-HO-MET (~15 mg) + Phenibut (1 g) — “Stream Liturgy”**  
> *0 h38: Ceiling lamp is definitely the moon.*  
> *1 h47: Language loses meaning; chair-creaks become music.*  
> *2 h12: Walked to forest stream, cried at nothing, returned  
> with overwhelming gratitude to parents.  10/10, will not  
> ingest again soon.*

Lesson → Low-dose wonder can reopen ordinary gratitude circuits
without burning the motherboard.  Keep this ratio sacred.

4 ░ Ritual Praxis & Domestic Thaumaturgy
────────────────────────────────────────
⚙︎ **The Kraken Heart Lamp**  
Materials: small LED cube (∞-mirror), extension cord, decibel
sensor.  
Action: place cube in common room; calibrate so it pulses when
ambient sound < 35 dB.  Silence → bioluminescence → collective
hush becomes communion.

⚙︎ **Transcendental Laundry Knot**  
Fold 7 magenta turtlenecks, 7 tanks, 14 black pant-legs into a
mobius-like braid.  Knot rests in closet corner; belief that
“clothes choose the wearer” increases dressing speed ≈ 30 %.

⚙︎ **Entropy-to-Order Sweep**  
Before finals week: sweep hallway clockwise while reciting
house motto (*Δ Order > 0 ∧ Δ Wonder > 0*).  Dump dust at base
of staircase; offer one gummy worm to the Ink-Loop.

*Safety Note:* Psychedelics optional.  Mop water alone can be
hallucinogenic at 3 A.M.

7 ░ Afterword: On Curating Our Own Haunting
───────────────────────────────────────────
Coulter House is no longer a staircase photo.  
It is a consent-based hallucination sustained by yearly intakes
of shy freshmen, faint rumors, and LED cube afterglow.

Your job is not to keep the myth *accurate*; it is to keep it
*alive*.  Paint new tentacles, redact the dates, mispronounce
the word “kraken” if that’s what cracks the shell.  The point
is motion.

If you ever feel lost, place your ear to the banister.
Somewhere underneath, a spore is still whispering seawater.
